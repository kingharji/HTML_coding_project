Royal Rasoi's Menu- A Culinary Journey Through India

We offer a variety of authentic dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and bursting with the vibrant flavors of India. Explore our menu and embark on a culinary adventure through different regions of the subcontinent.

    Appetizers (Starters):

  • Samosas (₹120):
    • Crispy golden fried pastries filled with spiced potatoes and peas, served with a tangy mint chutney.
  • Chicken Tikka (₹180):
    • Succulent marinated chicken pieces grilled to perfection in a tandoor oven, served with onions and bell peppers.
  • Veg Pakoras (₹150):
    • A medley of assorted vegetables dipped in a chickpea batter and deep-fried until golden, served with coriander chutney.
  • Dahi Vada (₹100):
    • Lentil dumplings soaked in creamy yogurt, drizzled with sweet tamarind chutney and sprinkled with crunchy sev (fried chickpea noodles).

    Main Courses:

  • Butter Chicken (₹250):
    • Tender chicken simmered in a rich tomato and cream sauce flavored with fragrant spices.
  • Saag Paneer (₹220):
    • Creamy spinach curry with cubes of fresh paneer (Indian cottage cheese) - a vegetarian delight.
  • Lamb Rogan Josh (₹300):
    • Slow-cooked lamb in a flavorful onion and tomato gravy with aromatic spices.
  • Chicken Biryani (₹280):
    • Fragrant basmati rice layered with tender chicken pieces, vegetables, and aromatic spices - a classic Indian dish.

    Desserts:

  • Gulab Jamun (₹100):
    • Soft, melt-in-your-mouth milk dumplings simmered in a sweet rosewater syrup.
  • Gajar ka Halwa (₹120):
    • A rich and creamy carrot pudding flavored with cardamom and nuts.
  • Kulfi (₹80):
    • Traditional Indian ice cream with a dense and creamy texture, available in various flavors (mango, pistachio, etc.).
  • Rasmalai (₹150):
    • Delicate cottage cheese dumplings soaked in a creamy, milk-based reduction flavored with cardamom and saffron.
